What is Color a Dice?

Color a Dice is an interactive color dice roller that lets you roll between 1 and 6 dice simultaneously. Each die displays one of six vibrant colors: yellow, orange, blue, red, green, and purple. Choose a custom background color, and the system intelligently excludes that color from appearing on any die — ensuring every result is always visible, distinct, and conflict-free against your chosen background.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about rolling with color a dice

How many dice can I roll at once?

You can roll between 1 and 6 dice simultaneously. Simply use the dice count selector to choose how many dice you want on the board, then hit the roll button to see the colors appear!

What colors can appear on the dice?

The dice feature six vibrant colors: Yellow, Orange, Blue, Red, Green, and Purple. Each die face displays one of these colors on every roll.

Why doesn't my background color appear on the dice?

That's our smart exclusion system at work! When you select a custom background color, our algorithm automatically removes that color from the dice pool. This ensures the dice always remain visually distinct and never blend into the background — creating a clean, conflict-free experience every time.

Can I use this on mobile?

Absolutely! color a dice is built as a fully responsive web application. Whether you're on a phone, tablet, or desktop, the dice roller adapts beautifully to your screen size. The touch-friendly controls make rolling on mobile just as fun as on desktop.

Is it free to use?

Yes! color a dice is completely free to use with no hidden costs, subscriptions, or in-app purchases. Just open the app and start rolling — it's that simple. We believe fun, interactive tools should be accessible to everyone.
